An introduction to vehicle physics

The PhysicsVehicle class (a subclass of PhysicsRigidBody) provides a simplified physics model for vehicles.

To associate a vehicle with a Spatial, there’s VehicleControl, a physics control that extends PhysicsVehicle.

Yet again, the physics control and its collision object are the same object.
